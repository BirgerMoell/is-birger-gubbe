## What's a Gubbe?

In Swedish culture, "gubbe" literally means "old man" but is used playfully to refer to men who have reached middle age (typically 40+). It comes with certain privileges:

- Authority to complain about weather
- Right to say "Back in my day..." unironically
- Wisdom of experience (or at least the appearance of it)
